Who are we?

A Black, Female and Youth Led NPO

OL Afrika Media Foundation, a 100% black, female and youth led Multimedia non-profit established in 2016. we are more than just a multimedia non-profit; we are a catalyst for change, empowerment, and cultural enrichment. With a firm commitment to Africa's vibrant narrative, Our mission is to nurture and elevate the next generation of creatives and storytellers, empowering them to share their voices on a global stage.
Through comprehensive training programs, internships, and learnerships, we provide young people with the skills and opportunities they need to succeed in diverse career paths, ranging from multimedia to business, finance and ICT opportunities that support our multimedia sector.
Our focus on youth development extends beyond skill-building; we are dedicated to instilling confidence and resilience in the Black child of South Africa, equipping them to navigate both local and international landscapes with ease. Central to our vision is the belief in the power of African stories to captivate and inspire audiences worldwide. Through our support of emerging musicians and creators, we ensure that their voices are heard and their talent recognized on international platforms. At the heart of our work lies our tagline, “Afrika to the World” , encapsulating our dedication to amplifying African narratives and fostering connections that transcend borders.


Mission and Vision

To improve the quality of life for underserved communities through philanthropic initiatives, strategic partnerships, and innovative solutions. We aim to address pressing social and environmental challenges while fostering sustainable change.

Is to create a world where every individual has equal access to opportunities, resources, and a sustainable environment. We aspire to be a catalyst for positive change, shaping a brighter and more equitable future for generations to come
